HOW TO PLAY
  1. The game is played on a grid that is 3x3
  2. One player will play X while the other will play O. Each will mark a box every turn, followed by the other.
  3. One player will win when three boxes are marked in a line, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally, with the same characters by the same player.
